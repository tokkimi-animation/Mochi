# Korea visual continuity - EP001

Use these references in the images while keeping the plush animated design.

## Everyday Korea details

- 24-hour convenience-store exterior, inspired by Korean 편의점 layouts but without real brand logos.
- Rainy residential street, apartment windows, power lines, street lamps, delivery scooter silhouette.
- Automatic sliding glass doors with visible sensor, entrance mat, umbrella stand, plastic umbrella sleeve stand.
- Triangle kimbap warmer, cup ramyeon shelves, hot-water ramyeon dispenser, chopstick dispenser.
- Banana milk bottles, canned coffee, warm drink shelf, snack aisle, small shopping basket.
- Recycle sorting bins, CCTV/security sticker, payment terminal/card sticker as background details only.
- Wet tile floor and puddle reflections; warm interior fluorescent lights against cool blue rainy night.

## Character continuity

- Mochi: white soft plush body, cream crescent horns, large orange-brown eyes, rosy cheeks, glowing chest star, tiny golden lights around him in emotional moments.
- Zumu: round blue plush body, orange eyes, tiny yellow forehead star, glowing blue comet tail, soft childlike expressions.
- Keep both characters chibi and plush, never geometric, never stick-like, never adult.

## Text rule

No text, no captions, no readable dialogue, no labels, no panel numbers, no watermark inside generated images.

Background signs may feel Korean through color, iconography, layout, and blurred Hangul-like shapes, but must not contain important readable story text. Dialogue is added later as a separate animated overlay.
